Privacy Policy

Active Dispatch (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We may request access to your device’s location to calculate distances to nearby incidents. Location data is used only within the app to provide this functionality and is not shared or stored externally.

How We Use Information

• To show you incidents relative to your current location.
• To improve app performance and reliability.
• To ensure a personalized user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Your location is never transmitted to external servers, except as needed to request nearby incident data from our backend services.

Your Choices

You can deny or revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings. If disabled, the app will fall back to city-level data only.

Disclaimer

Active Dispatch is not an official emergency service and should not be relied upon as a replacement for contacting 911, local police, or other emergency providers. Always seek official sources for urgent or emergency information.
