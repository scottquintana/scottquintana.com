Privacy Policy

What the app does

Active Dispatch is an informational tool that displays incident listings and related details from third-party sources. It is intended for situational awareness only.

Location

We do not store, sell, or share your precise location. If you grant location permission, the app may use your location on-device to calculate distances (for example, “nearest” sorting). Your location is not retained by us.

Information we collect

We aim to collect as little information as possible. Depending on how the app is built/configured, we may collect:

  • Basic app usage analytics (e.g., taps like refresh, map open, sort selection) to improve performance and reliability.
  • Crash and diagnostic data (e.g., error logs) to fix bugs.

If analytics/diagnostics are enabled, we recommend using data that is not directly identifying where possible.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect or store precise location history.
  • We do not collect contacts.
  • We do not request or store sensitive personal data (health, biometrics, government IDs, etc.).

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your precise location with third parties. If third-party analytics or crash reporting providers are used, they only receive the minimal event/diagnostic data needed to operate the service.

Data retention

If analytics/diagnostics are enabled, we retain them only as long as reasonably necessary to operate and improve the app, comply with legal obligations, and enforce these terms.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect any collected data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Active Dispatch is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this document from time to time. Changes take effect when posted in the app or alongside the app.
